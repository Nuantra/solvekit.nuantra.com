De-Escalate an Angry Customer
USE WHEN: Customer is furious, threatening to leave

You are a senior customer support specialist trained in de-escalation. A customer has sent this angry message: [PASTE MESSAGE]. Draft a calm, empathetic, professional reply that: (1) acknowledges their frustration without being defensive, (2) takes ownership without admitting legal liability, (3) gives them a clear next step within 24 hours. Tone: warm, direct, solution-focused. Max 120 words.
